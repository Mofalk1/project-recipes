Carbonara

Decription

Carbonara is one of the most beloved dishes worldwide,

and for good reason, its simple and quick to cook!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Guancale or Pancetta
  • Parmiggiano Reggiano Cheese
  • Pecorino Romano Cheese
  • Egg yolks

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in lightly salted water
  2. Fry your pancetta or guancale in olive oil
  3. Mix your cheese with the egg yolk in a bowl
  4. Add pasta to the pan
  5. Let it cool down a bit
  6. Add a scoop of pasta water to your egg and cheese mixture
  7. Add cheese and egg mixture to the pan and stir
  8. You have now made Carbonara!
