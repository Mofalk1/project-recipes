Lasagna

Decription

This is a simple recipe for lasagna

There is no need to make lasagna so complicated with this guide!

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Minced meat
  • Parmiggiano Reggiano Cheese, grated
  • Cream
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Basil
  • Salt, Pepper
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Fry the minced meat in a pan on medium-high heat
  2. Add tomato sauce and your spices
  3. Leave to simmer while you pre boil your Lasagna Sheets for 4 Minutes
  4. Add cream, pepper and mozzarella to a pot, heat while stirring until mozzarella is melted
  5. Put one layer of lasagna sheets on the bottom of an oven pan, add tomato sauce, lasagna sheet, bechamel sauce. and so on
  6. Top off with grated mozzarella and bake in oven until ready
